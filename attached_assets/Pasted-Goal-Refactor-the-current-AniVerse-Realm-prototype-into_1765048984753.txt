Goal:
Refactor the current AniVerse Realm prototype into a clean structure with a new “Cards” hub that includes Gacha, Collection, Market, and Offers, plus clearly organized sections and subsections for the whole app.

Keep the existing cyberpunk / anime design and components, but reorganize pages, routes, and navigation as described below.
