REPLIT BUILD PROMPT — Day-0 Onboarding (60-Second Win)

Goal:
Implement a lightweight onboarding flow that gives new users a “win” in under 60 seconds,
increases first-session retention, and disappears once completed.

────────────────────────
A) DETECTION
────────────────────────
- Add user flag: onboarding_completed (boolean, default false).
- On Home page load:
  - If onboarding_completed == false → show onboarding widget.
  - If true → never show again.

────────────────────────
B) ONBOARDING WIDGET (HOME PAGE)
────────────────────────
Placement:
- At the top of Home page (above Rewards).

UI:
Title: “Welcome to Aurelith”
Subtitle: “Complete your first steps to enter the realm.”

Checklist (render as steps with checkmarks):

[ ] 1. Claim your free summon  
[ ] 2. Earn your first badge  
[ ] 3. Share your first pull (optional)

Rules:
- Steps auto-complete based on real actions (no manual buttons).
- Step 3 is optional and should not block completion.

────────────────────────
C) STEP LOGIC
────────────────────────

Step 1 — Claim your free summon
- Auto-complete when user performs their first summon (daily free or paid).
- If user clicks “Claim Free Summon” from Rewards, this also counts.

Step 2 — Earn your first badge
- On first summon completion, auto-grant badge:
  Badge code: REALMWALKER_I
  Name: “Realmwalker I”
  Description: “Completed your first summon in AniRealm.”
- Mark step complete when badge is granted.

Step 3 — Share your first pull (optional)
- Complete if user shares a summon post via “Share to Feed”.
- If skipped, onboarding can still complete after step 2.

────────────────────────
D) COMPLETION BEHAVIOR
────────────────────────
- When step 1 + step 2 are completed:
  - Set onboarding_completed = true.
  - Collapse onboarding widget into a small banner:
    “Onboarding complete ✓ Welcome to the realm.”
  - Banner is dismissible and disappears permanently when dismissed.

────────────────────────
E) SAFETY & UX
────────────────────────
- No personal info.
- Works for minors.
- No forced posting.
- No blocking modals.

────────────────────────
ACCEPTANCE TESTS
────────────────────────
1) New user sees onboarding widget on Home.
2) After first summon, step 1 + 2 complete automatically.
3) “Realmwalker I” badge appears on profile.
4) Onboarding widget collapses and never returns.
5) Optional share does not block completion.
