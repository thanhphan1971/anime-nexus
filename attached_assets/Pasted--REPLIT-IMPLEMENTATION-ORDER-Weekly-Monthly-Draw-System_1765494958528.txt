✅ REPLIT IMPLEMENTATION ORDER
Weekly & Monthly Draw System (Dynamic, Safe, Minor-Friendly)
1️⃣ SYSTEM OVERVIEW (LOCKED)

The app includes two separate draw systems:

🎁 Weekly Draw

Purpose: Retention, goodwill, habit

Entry: Free (manual)

Winners: Many (dynamic scaling)

Rewards: Small, cosmetic, non-progression

🌟 Monthly Draw

Purpose: Excitement, aspiration, community moment

Entry: Manual

Winners: Few

Rewards: Bigger and more exciting than weekly, but not power-breaking

Draws are not gacha, do not affect card odds, and are optional.

2️⃣ WEEKLY DRAW — RULES
📅 Reset

Resets every Monday at 00:00 UTC

Same reset time for all users

🎟️ Entry Rules

User must log in at least once during the week

Entry is manual (user clicks “Enter Weekly Draw”)

Base entries:

Free users: 1

Premium users: 3

Bonus entries:

“Bonus Weekly Entry” item adds +1 entry

No automatic entry

🏆 Weekly Winner Scaling (Dynamic)

Metric: Weekly Active Users (WAU)

Formula:

weekly_winners = clamp(round(WAU × 0.05), min=30, max=200)


Winner count increases as the active user base grows

Each account can win at most once per week

🎁 Weekly Prize Table (Fixed Quality)

Reward quality never scales, only the number of winners.

Distribution (scale proportionally):

Reward	%	Notes
50 Tokens	40%	Small economy fuel
100 Tokens	20%	Still modest
Cosmetic Badge / Frame	20%	Visual only
Avatar Effect (7 days)	10%	Temporary cosmetic
1 Common Card	5%	Common tier only
Bonus Weekly Entry	5%	+1 entry next week

Hard rules:

❌ No Rare or higher cards

❌ No power boosts

❌ No paid-only weekly draws

3️⃣ MONTHLY DRAW — RULES
📅 Reset

Resets on the 1st of each month at 00:00 UTC

🎟️ Entry Rules

Entry is manual

Entries do not auto-renew

User Type	Entry Access
Premium users	1 free monthly entry
Free users	Entry via tokens or events

Monthly entry must always feel optional.

4️⃣ MONTHLY WINNER STRUCTURE (BIGGER, BUT CONTROLLED)
🥇 Grand Winner (1 user)

Rotate one of the following per month:

🎴 1 Epic Card (maximum rarity allowed)

💰 1,000 Tokens

🎨 Exclusive Monthly Cosmetic Set

👑 30-Day Premium Pass

This is the headline prize.

🥈 Secondary Winners (10 users)

Each secondary winner receives one of the following:

💰 100–300 Tokens (MAX 300 tokens)

🎨 High-quality cosmetic item

👑 7-Day Premium Pass

Hard rule:

❌ Secondary winners may NOT receive Epic cards

❌ Token rewards for secondary winners may NOT exceed 300 tokens

🥉 Optional Participation Reward (Small)

Optional and minimal:

10–25 tokens or

Sticker / badge

5️⃣ WEEKLY vs MONTHLY — COMPARISON (INTENTIONAL)
Aspect	Weekly	Monthly
Winners	30–200 (dynamic)	11 total
Max Tokens	100	1,000
Cards	Common only	Epic (1 winner max)
Cosmetics	Basic	Exclusive
Emotional Impact	“Nice”	“WOW”

Monthly prizes are 3–5× more exciting, not more powerful.

6️⃣ VERIFICATION & ELIGIBILITY RULES (MANDATORY)
Minimum requirements to WIN a draw:

Account age ≥ 24 hours

Email address verified

One win per account per draw period

Notes:

Users may enter draws before meeting requirements

Users who do not meet requirements simply cannot win yet

No warnings or error messages shown

7️⃣ ABUSE PREVENTION (MINIMAL & SAFE)

No device fingerprinting in beta

No phone number requirement

No CAPTCHA

Controls used:

Account age check

Email verification

One win per account per period

Manual entry only

This approach is minor-safe, Québec-friendly, and App Store compliant.

8️⃣ UI TRANSPARENCY REQUIREMENTS
Weekly Draw UI

🎉 This week: {{weekly_winners}} winners
Winners increase as the community grows.

Monthly Draw UI

🌟 Monthly Grand Draw
🥇 1 Grand Winner
🥈 10 Secondary Winners
Rewards are optional and do not affect progression.

9️⃣ BACKEND FLOW (PLAIN LOGIC)
Weekly Draw

Count Weekly Active Users

Compute number of winners using scaling formula

Validate eligible entries

Randomly select winners

Assign rewards from fixed weekly table

Notify winners

Monthly Draw

Collect valid entries

Select 1 grand winner

Select 10 secondary winners

Assign rewards respecting max limits

Announce winners (opt-in display)
